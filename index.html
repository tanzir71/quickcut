What is QuickCut?

QuickCut uses offline VOSK speech recognition to detect spoken words, remove silence and filler words, and automatically stitch the best parts of your video. Configure thresholds, fades, and output formats via a clean, cross‑platform interface.

Silence Detection

Automatically finds and removes pauses for tighter videos.

Filler Word Cleanup

ASR timestamps trim "um", "uh", and similar fillers from speech.

Offline & Private

Runs VOSK locally—no uploads, no cloud processing.
